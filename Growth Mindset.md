# Growth Mindset

##   Introduction

In this module, we will be studying various technical skills related to programming and software development. However, success in this field is not just about technical proficiency - it also heavily relies on having the right mindset and emotional intelligence to deal with challenges and uncertainty. This prework is designed to help us cultivate a growth mindset, develop grit and resilience, and become more aware of our biases and emotional reactions.

## Assigned Resources

-   **Upgrade Your Technical Skills with Deliberate Practice**  (Article)
    -   This article explains the concept of deliberate practice, which involves breaking down complex skills into smaller components and practicing them in a focused and structured way. The author argues that deliberate practice is the key to improving one's technical skills, and that it requires discipline, feedback, and a willingness to step outside one's comfort zone.
-   **Carol Dweck on the Growth Mindset**  (Video)
    -   In this TED Talk, Carol Dweck explains the difference between a fixed mindset (the belief that one's abilities are predetermined and unchangeable) and a growth mindset (the belief that one's abilities can be developed through effort and learning). She argues that having a growth mindset leads to better learning, motivation, and resilience.
-   **Angela Lee Duckworth on Grit**  (Video)
    -   In this TED Talk, Angela Lee Duckworth defines grit as "perseverance and passion for long-term goals". She explains that grit is a better predictor of success than talent or intelligence, and that it can be developed through deliberate practice, a sense of purpose, and a growth mindset.
-   **Alain de Botton on Redefining Success**  (Video)
    -   In this TED Talk, Alain de Botton argues that our culture's definition of success is too narrow and focused on external markers such as wealth, fame, and status. He suggests that we should redefine success as the achievement of meaningful goals that align with our values and passions.
