# Engineering Readings

## Discussion Questions:

### 1. What’s the one thing I bring to this career (and a potential employer) that nobody else can?

Each individual has unique strengths and experiences that they can bring to a potential employer. Reflecting on personal strengths and skills can help in identifying what sets one apart from others. It can be anything from creativity, attention to detail, leadership skills, or a unique perspective on problem-solving.

### 2. What are 3 things I’ll start doing to “un-stick” myself whenever I get stuck on tough piece of code, logic, or feature?

When facing a tough problem, it's important to have strategies to get "un-stuck" and move forward. Some things that one can do include:

1.  Taking a break and coming back to the problem later with fresh eyes.
2.  Breaking down the problem into smaller, more manageable parts.
3.  Asking for help or seeking feedback from peers or mentors.
4.  Trying a different approach or brainstorming new ideas.
5.  Researching similar problems or looking for examples online.
